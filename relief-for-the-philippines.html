About Samaritan’s Purse and World Vision

Samaritan’s Purse

http://www.samaritanspurse.org

MISSION STATEMENT
Samaritan’s Purse is a nondenominational evangelical Christian organization providing spiritual and physical aid to hurting people around the world. Since 1970, Samaritan’s Purse has helped meet needs of people who are victims of war, poverty, natural disasters, disease, and famine with the purpose of sharing God’s love through His Son, Jesus Christ.
The organization serves the Church worldwide to promote the Gospel of the Lord Jesus Christ.
Philippines Emergency Relief
Samaritan’s Purse supports local ministry partners in the region as they provide physical help to suffering people in Jesus’ Name. We are currently bringing desperately needed relief and comfort to families affected by Super Typhoon Haiyan.

World Vision

http://www.worldvision.org/

World Vision responding to Typhoon Haiyan devastation in the Philippines
Typhoon Haiyan, considered one of the strongest tropical storms ever, made landfall in the Philippines early November 8 and caused catastrophic damage. World Vision’s teams are assessing the damage and mobilizing a response.
